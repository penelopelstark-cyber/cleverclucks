About Us

Proudly hen-run since day one.

Here, our Clever Clucks well and truly rule the roost.

With freedom to do whatever they like, whenever they like, they’re often found roaming our private back lawn. As a result, they eat when they want, drink at will, sleep, forage, and dust bath at their leisure, and operate strictly on hen time — especially on darker mornings, when a sleep-in is simply non-negotiable.

We’re happy to indulge their relaxed approach to the working day, because once they’re finally up and about, they deliver every time — their famously
“Cracking Good Eggs”.

In return for keeping our community well supplied (and graciously accepting full credit for their popularity), we provide free food, cosy lodgings, and a generous playground they charge into each morning like it’s the social event of the season.

Our happy girls take their role very seriously — producing the tastiest eggs they possibly can — even if they insist on doing it entirely on their own terms.

Management would like to remind everyone that all complaints regarding late availability of eggs, should be directed to the hens.
